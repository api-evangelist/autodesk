Data Exchange in Model Coordination - Private Beta Lejla Secerbegovic 29 May 2026 Lejla Secerbegovic Share Data Exchange helps teams share and use the right project data across Autodesk and third-party tools without having to exchange entire models. Instead of moving large files back and forth, teams can create focused, structured exchanges that contain only the relevant data needed for a specific workflow. This makes collaboration more efficient, reduces duplication, and helps project stakeholders work with up-to-date information across connected tools.
