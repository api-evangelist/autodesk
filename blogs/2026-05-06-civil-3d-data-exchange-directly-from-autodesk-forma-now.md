Civil 3D Data Exchange Directly from Autodesk Forma - Now Available in Public Beta Gaurav Bhamre 8 May 2026 Gaurav Bhamre Share Great news for Data Exchange enthusiasts and Civil 3D and Revit® designers! Over the last year and during AU 2025 we saw great interest in the Data Exchange connector for Civil 3D from team working on Infrastructure projects. So, we are bringing this capability directly into your Forma files, same as our Revit and IFC connector. With this connector you can directly create exchanges from your Civil 3D files hosted on Forma.
