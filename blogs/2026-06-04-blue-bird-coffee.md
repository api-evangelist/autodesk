Blue Bird Coffee, a Colombian coffee producer, used Autodesk Platform Services and digital twin technology to address climate change impacts on their farm. By leveraging Forma Site Design for tree-planting simulations, the family achieved a 68% reduction in coffee berry borer infestations, increased shade coverage from 29.5% to 42.2%, and improved bean quality scores.
