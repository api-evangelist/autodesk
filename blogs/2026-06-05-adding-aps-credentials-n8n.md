This blog post explains how to connect n8n, a low-code/no-code automation platform, with Autodesk Forma using APS (Autodesk Platform Services) APIs. The article focuses on authentication setup, guiding users through configuring OAuth2 credentials by saving the OAuth Redirect URL as a Callback URL in their application for proper integration between the two platforms.
