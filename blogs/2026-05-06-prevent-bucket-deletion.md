Prevent bucket deletion adam.nagy 8 May 2026 Adam Nagy Share There was a time a while ago when multiple companies reported that they accidentally deleted a bucket and asked us to restore its content. Unfortunately, we do not have a way to restore the content of deleted buckets. Therefore, it's very important for you to make sure that the bucket you're deleting and all its contents are not needed.
